    Oman's Message of Islam

    The Sultanate of Oman has been been sharing its peaceful philosophy at home and abroad for centuries. Oman's Ministry of Endowments and Religious Affairs has been promoting interfaith dialogue for decades... read the Minister's message here >>>

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to promote interfaith dialogue, present Oman's vision of tolerant Islam, and show the whole world how Omanis have practiced this coexistence for hundreds of years, inside and outside Oman.

    Our Plan

    Our plan is to spread our initiative "Tolerance, Understanding and Coexistence" around the world through exhibitions, workshops, seminars, coloring books for children and lots of online activities.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is that hatred, discrimination, prejudice, double standards, inequality, violence and injustice due to misinterpreted religious texts will disappear completely from our beloved planet.
