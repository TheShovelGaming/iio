    Tolerance . Understanding . Coexistence . Oman's Message of Islam

    The Sultanate of Oman has been sharing its peaceful philosophy at home and abroad for centuries. Oman's Ministry of Endowments and Religious Affairs has been promoting interfaith dialogue for decades... read the Minister's message here >>>

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to promote interfaith dialogue, present Oman's vision of tolerant Islam, and show the whole world how Omanis have practiced this coexistence for hundreds of years, equally inside and outside Oman.

    Our Plan

    Our plan is to spread Oman's message of Islam; "Tolerance, Understanding and Coexistence", all over the globe by organizing exhibitions, seminars and conferences, distributing coloring books for kids and many other online activities.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to actively participate in the complete eradication of discrimination, inequality, prejudice, double standards, hatred, violence and injustice due to misinterpreted religious texts from our beloved planet.

    Iinterfaith Dialogue

    For many years the government of Oman has been promoting interfaith dialogue to foster religious tolerance, mutual understanding, and peaceful coexistence on a global scale.
